How To Play

Your Character: You control an orange rectangle

  • The orange rectangle follows your cursor
  • Hold R to start spinning. You must be spinning to deal damage!
  • Press Esc to pause and E for shop

Goal: Eliminate all the pink rectangles by running into them

  • To eliminate a pink rectangle, run into it while spinning
  • The pink rectangles run away from you
  • As the rounds go on, pink rectangles will have more and more health

Enemies: Enemy rectangles deal damage to you!

  • Poisonous rectangles are green. Touching them damages you
  • Pulsing rectangles are purple and red. When they are red, a red circle appears that damages you if you are inside it
  • Turret rectangles are blue and red. When they are red, they fire bullets that do damage
  • Enemy rectangles deal more damage as the rounds go on

Shop: Buy items and stat upgrades from the shop!

  • Grenade: eliminates all enemy rectangles in a large radius. Press 1 to use
  • Shield: grants invincibility for 8 seconds. Press 2 to use
  • Bomb: automatically wins the round. Press 3 to use
  • Health: Increases your health
  • Damage: Increases your damage
  • Everything gets more expensive the more you buy!

Math Mode: You can turn math mode on or off by going to the home screen

  • If Math Mode is on, there will be an addition, multiplication, subtract, or division problem at the end of each round
